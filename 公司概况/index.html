    哞哞国际贸易

            哞哞国际贸易（上海）有限公司是一家专注于进口肉类贸易的公司，是牧场控股集团旗下全资子公司。主营业务是冷冻牛肉、羊肉、鹿肉、禽类及农副产品的进口，并为食品工厂、大型餐饮、电子商务、超市、批发市场等提供货源。

            Moumou International Trading is a company that focuses on meat import and trading, and is also a Wholly-owned subsidiary of muchang Holding Group. Main businesses are the import of frozen beef、 lamb、venison、poultry、agricultural and sideline products. More, we provide food factories、large restaurants、 e-commerce、 supermarkets with source of goods.
